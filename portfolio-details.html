Data-driven conformity assessment and import inspection systems

Leinad has led and supported digital systems for the PVoC programme, including certification databases, digital monitoring and evaluation systems, KPI dashboards, risk profiling, intelligence analytics, and enriched Certificate of Conformity data workflows. The work supports evidence-based regulatory decisions, partner oversight, secure data handling, and continuous improvement aligned with standards such as ISO 30401 and ISO/IEC 27001.
